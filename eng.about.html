Baltic Business Development is a limited liability company, that Is based and headquartered in Georgia, partnering with some of the leading companies in Europe.
BBD works globally, with governments and private entities, but some of our best work still remains within Georgia, like one of the biggest malls in Tbilisi Uptwon Shopping Center in Tbilisi’, the famous Balneological Spa center in Tskaltubo, and Ministry of Justice within Tbilisi again. The end-result is evidence of our quality of work.
From the projects that we have taken on, few larger scale projects stand out as especially complex and interesting challenges, like the Biltimore hotel, Casino Jewel, various projects done for the Silk-Road Group, as well as Dariali Energy and Devdoraki Tunnel in Dariali.
